Frequently Asked Questions

Trust me, you are not the only one who ask that same question over and over again.

Are you an agent just trying to get a listing?

Although we are licensed real estate agents, we try to keep the two businesses separated. We have the two sides of buying homes ourselves and selling homes for other people. We will pursue whatever avenue seems to help our client out the most with what they need.

How can you save me money?

This goes back to the listing your home question. When we buy your property we do not take a commission, which is typically 6% of the sales price. You will only be responsible for your excise tax (1.78% right now), title and half of escrow. You will not have to get the home ready for open houses and all that goes along with the tradition sale of a home. We buy our homes in as-is condition. No repairs needed.

Do you buy real estate all over the state?

Although we do look to buy everywhere (have purchased in Ballard area) we are focusing our efforts mostly in Bellingham, WA. Specifically the Cornwall, Columbia, Sunnyland, Sehome Hill, York and Letter Street districts. But we will look at anything.

Do you only buy single family homes?

No! We are very interested in multi-family real estate as well and have quite a few in our portfolio already and very familiar with commercial real estate.

Do you pay all cash for your properties?

That is always an option but not always the best option for everyone. We have done cash out’s, lease options and owner contracts. All have a place in the business and serve a purpose. It all depends on what works best for you as a seller!

Are you a large company?

We are a small company that provides custom transactions. We are much more flexible and open to discussion than a large real estate investment company.
