Our Amazing Team

Diskin Young
Founder & CEO

I became interested in Real Estate investing at a very early age. Around middle school I decided that is what I wanted to pursue in life. I started reading Rich Dad, Poor Dad and other books dedicate to the world of Real Estate investment. At my young age I have to prove my worth and prove that I am knowledgeable in this area of business. I am hungry, motivated and energized to show that I have what it takes to be a successful Real Estate Investor.

I graduated from Western Washington University in 2014 from the College of Business and Economics with a focus in economics. Graduating from a highly ranked business school taught me to look for answers and find a solution, and to not use excuses. It taught me to work with others and to collaborate, and to not caring about who gets the credit, only caring about getting the win.I also learned to think outside the box, giving up or failure is not an option. Another big life lesson for me was being diagnosed with cancer at the age of 16. I had to grow up fast and take on life’s challenges head on and I now know I can fight through anything. More education came from one of my personal mentors Greg Pinneo. He is a Real Estate investor out of Seattle, WA and works through his company “The Cor Company.” I have gone to countless classes that would be considered Masters Classes in any Business School. Along with that I have listened to approximately 200 hours of education that he provides.

Things I enjoy other than reading and learning about Real Estate is sports. I’ve played basketball and tennis throughout my life. I also enjoy working out at Bellingham Fitness, swimming, biking and being with friends and family. I believe you will see my expertise right away!

Monte Young
CFO

Monte Young has been in the real estate business for over 25 years now and has encountered and dealt with all the complex issues that the world of real estate can throw at you. He is a constant professional and works harder for the person to have an enjoyable experience rather than trying to get a quick paycheck. Monte is not only one of Whatcom County’s top real estate agents but is also one of the largest real estate investors in town. He has completed 14 buy, rehab and sell projects many happening at the same time and owns and manages over 50 rental units of various sizes in Bellingham. He has well over 1,000 hours of accumulated real estate education.

For his personal life he married Lisa, his high school sweetheart in 1985 and has 3 Children, Diskin, Hannah and Colton. He is a big contributor to various organizations like the Salvation Army and the American Cancer Society. Also, coached basketball, football, soccer and baseball for many years with the boys and girls club. In his free time he enjoys hiking, competitive sailing, road biking, motorcycles, tennis and volleyball. You will see his commitment to your transaction instantly.
